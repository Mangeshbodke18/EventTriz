FAQs

Q: How far in advance should I book an event?
A: We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for major events like weddings, and 1-2 months in advance for smaller events.

Q: Can I customize my event package?
A: Yes, all our packages are fully customizable to match your requirements. Contact us to discuss your preferences.

Q: What happens in case of cancellation?
A: We offer flexible cancellation policies, but cancellations within 15 days of the event may incur a cancellation fee.
